Commodities

Raw commodities examples of agricultural origin are: cotton, peanuts, cereals (rice, maize, wheat, barley), oil, bananas, rubber, cocoa, coffee, lumber, among others. In minerals, raw commodities are traditional coal, natural gas, iron ore and oil.

The processed commodities are those that have some industrial processing, to end usrs or for use in other industries. Examples of processed agricultural and minerals, commodities are: rice received, sharps in general, soy oil, sugar, cotton yarn, meat, timber, pulp, concetrate frozen orange juice, oil products, aluminum, and so on. In all these levels and according to the country needs to meet conditions and needs, of course, a prior consultation is necessary.
